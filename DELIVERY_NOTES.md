# Delivery notes

## Milestones

- Project scaffolded as a Next.js and TypeScript site.
- Research content transformed into structured JSON.
- Industrial dossier design direction selected after comparing dark engineering and light technical directions.
- DESIGN.md created for tokens, typography, layout and component rules.
- Core pages implemented: home, about, services, engineering services, projects, certifications, contact, careers and 404.
- Static contact form added with accessible labels and a live status message.
- QA, accessibility, performance and security notes added.

## Design decisions

The selected direction is a light technical dossier: off-white substrate, navy authority panels, dark green accents, sharp geometry and gridded service tiles. It is intentionally not a SaaS-style gradient site.

## Placeholder policy

Facts from the research file are used directly. Missing biographies, project details, email address, policy text, case study outcomes and job details are marked as client content required.
